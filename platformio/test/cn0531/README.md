# CN0531 connected to nucleo_l432kc using Arduino-AD57X1 library.
Output 3 different tension every 3 seconds.

#### Connection

 nucleo_l432k  |  CN0531   | Purpose
---------------|-----------|--------------
 D10           |    1      | CSS/SYNC_N
 D11	       |    2      | MOSI/SDIN
 D12	       |    3 	   | MISO/SDO
 D13	       |    4	   | SCK/SCLK
 GND           |    5      | GND/AGND
 3V3           |    6      | VCC/IOVCC
