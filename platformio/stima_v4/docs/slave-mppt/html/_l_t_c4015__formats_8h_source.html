2LTC4015: Multichemistry Buck Battery Charger Controller with Digital Telemetry System
3
4@verbatim
5The LTC®4015 is a complete synchronous buck controller/charger with pin
6selectable, chemistry specific charging and termination algorithms. The LTC4015
7can charge Li-Ion/Polymer, LiFePO4, or leadacid batteries. Battery charge
8voltage is pin selectable and I²C adjustable. Input current limit and charge
9current can be accurately programmed with sense resistors and can be
10individually adjusted via the I²C serial port. A digital telemetry system
11monitors all system power parameters. Safety timer and current termination
12algorithms are supported for lithium chemistry batteries. The LTC4015 also
13includes automatic recharge, precharge (Li-Ion) and NTC thermistor protection.
14The LTC4015's I²C port allows user customization of charger algorithms, reading
15of charger status information, configuration of the maskable and programmable
16alerts, plus use and configuration of the Coulomb counter. Available in a
1738-Lead 5mm × 7mm QFN package.
18@endverbatim
