Detailed Description

CV_TIMER_BF Bit Field.

For lithium chemistries, indicates the time (in sec) that the battery has been in constant-voltage regulation

  • Register: CV_TIMER
  • CommandCode: 49
  • Size: 16
  • Offset: 0
  • MSB: 15
  • MASK: 0xFFFF
  • Access: R
  • Default: n/a
