Detailed Description

ABSORB_TIMER_BF Bit Field.

For LiFePO4 and lead-acid batteries, indicates the time (in sec) that the battery has been in absorb phase

  • Register: ABSORB_TIMER
  • CommandCode: 50
  • Size: 16
  • Offset: 0
  • MSB: 15
  • MASK: 0xFFFF
  • Access: R
  • Default: n/a
